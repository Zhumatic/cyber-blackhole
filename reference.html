Cyber Blackhole


Purposes

Establish a system that is capable of effectively mornitoring the web accessibilty of users inside mainland China, collect, analyze and present the data in a way that could assist investors and other stakeholders in decision making.

Background

With China's growing economic power, its policy making/adjustment brings enormous impact on global market. At the same sime, growing commercial entities are becoming dependent on China's domestic market.

However, China's policy making process remains opaque compring with western counterparts, and the enforcement of policy often detached from official legal/policy documents discloased, and also subject to sudden change.

The control of information flow is often employed by Chinese adminstration for execution of policy. For example, when crypoto currency was declared illegal, platfroms for crypto trading and data were also being blocked inside China. Following the logic, by analyzing the data of web accessibility, the sysem able to sensor possible policy change in various sectors, acting as a complement for traditional market indicators.

Guide

Accessibility Description
◉ Chinese Sites Sites/services operated by enteties located within mainland China(with the exception of TikTok).
◉ Available Sites operated by enteties outside mainland China with unrestricted access.
◉ Blocked Sites operated by enteties outside mainland China which are blocked of access.
◉ Restricted Sites operated by enteties outside mainland China (with the exception of TikTok) that provide specilized versions of services to users in mainland China, or the serives of which are partially blocked.
◉ IP Denied Sites operated by enteties outside mainland China which deny users with mainland China IPs from accessing.
Categerization of Web Sites Description
Portal Sites offering combination of services, or search engines.
Commercial Sites offering service online for commercial/profit reasons.
Trading Sites acting as plaftform for b2c or c2c trading.
Sharing Platform for sharing of content or social networks.
Newswire News agency or press companies.
Entertainment Sites realted to products or services of leisure.
Utility Governmental or other sites for non-profit purposes.
Illegal Sites providing services which are in contradiction with mainland China's laws.
